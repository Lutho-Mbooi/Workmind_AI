# Workmind_AI
An AI-powered productivity assistant that helps professionals write better emails, summarise meeting notes, plan tasks, research topics, and get instant answers — all from a single, elegant interface.
WorkMind AI is a full-stack AI workplace assistant built for modern professionals who need intelligent, reliable support across their daily work. Whether you're drafting a formal stakeholder email, untangling a chaotic set of meeting notes, or trying to plan a productive week, WorkMind AI gives you a single tool that handles it all.

The application is powered by Claude (claude-sonnet-4-6) via Anthropic's API and is built with a React + TypeScript + Supabase stack. It features five integrated AI tools, a live chatbot interface, a unified dashboard, and a full Responsible AI policy — making it both a functional productivity suite and a demonstration of ethical AI development.

This project was built as part of a CAPACITI NQF Level 5 formative assessment exploring the practical application of AI in professional environments.

Knowledge workers spend significant time on repetitive writing, information synthesis, and scheduling — tasks that are mentally taxing but do not require deep expertise. These tasks are exactly where AI can add the most value. WorkMind AI addresses this by placing a capable AI assistant directly inside the professional's workflow, removing friction and allowing them to focus on work that actually requires human judgment

 Features

1. 📧 Smart Email Generator

Generate professional, context-aware emails in seconds. Choose from five tones — Formal, Friendly, Persuasive, Apologetic, or Follow-Up — and specify length from brief to detailed. Ideal for outreach, client communication, internal messaging, and negotiations.

2. 📋 Meeting Notes Summariser

Paste raw meeting notes and extract a clean summary, action items, key decisions, and deadlines — all in separate, copyable cards. Turns 30 minutes of messy notes into a structured, shareable document instantly.

3. 📅 AI Task Planner

Input your task list and generate a daily schedule, weekly plan, or Eisenhower Priority Matrix. The planner accounts for working hours, constraints, and task priorities — turning a disorganised to-do list into a realistic action plan.

4. 🔍 AI Research Assistant

Enter any topic or question and receive a structured summary, key insights, and recommendations. Supports four modes: Topic Overview, Article Summary, Pros & Cons Analysis, and Recommendations. Ideal for quick research, briefing preparation, and decision support.

5. 💬 AI Chat Interface

An interactive workplace assistant that handles any prompt — from drafting talking points to explaining frameworks. Includes suggested starter prompts, a conversation history display, and a clean chat UI that mirrors modern messaging apps.

 Usage Guide

Email Generator


Navigate to Email Generator in the sidebar.
Enter the recipient name, your name/role, and the email's purpose.
Select a tone (e.g., Formal) and length (e.g., Standard).
Click Generate Email.
Review the output, then copy or regenerate.


Notes Summariser


Navigate to Notes Summariser.
Paste your raw meeting notes into the input area.
Toggle on the output sections you need (Action Items, Decisions, Deadlines).
Click Summarise Notes.
Copy individual sections or the full summary.


Task Planner


Navigate to Task Planner.
List your tasks (one per line) in the input area.
Choose a planning mode: Daily Schedule, Weekly Plan, or Priority Matrix.
Set your working hours and any constraints.
Click Generate My Plan to receive a structured schedule.


Research Assistant


Navigate to Research Assistant.
Enter a topic, question, or URL.
Select an output mode (Topic Overview, Article Summary, etc.).
Click Research to generate insights and recommendations.


AI Chat


Navigate to AI Chat.
Type any workplace question or task into the input bar.
Use the suggested prompts to get started quickly.
Review and act on the AI's response.
